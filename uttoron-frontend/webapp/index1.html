FAQ

Questions? We've got answers.

Everything you need to know about your Skills Passport.

Do I need an email or phone number to sign up?+
No. Just pick a nickname, choose an avatar, and set a 4-digit PIN — that's your whole passport.
What is my "passport number"?+
When you create your passport, we generate a unique number like UT-4821. Use it together with your PIN to sign back in.
What happens if I forget my PIN?+
There's currently no PIN-recovery flow built yet — write it down somewhere safe. TODO: replace with a real recovery process or support contact once one exists.
How many lessons are there?+
Two tracks — Office & Data and Digital Presence — each with several lessons. Some are live now, more are coming soon.
Does Uttoron work on my phone?+
Yes — it works on any device with a browser, no installation required.
Is my data private?+
Everything is stored locally in your own browser. Nothing is sent to a server.
